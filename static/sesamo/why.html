Sesamo

Why Sesamo?

The Problem

Microsoft account verification is everywhere - Xbox, Office 365, Azure, and more. But there's one annoying step: every time you log in on a new device, you need to manually type an 8-character verification code.

The code appears on your screen, and you have to carefully type it on your phone. One wrong character? Start over.

The Solution

Sesamo eliminates this friction. Just point your camera at the code, or take a screenshot and select it. The app instantly recognizes the code using OCR technology and auto-fills it for you.

What used to take 30 seconds of careful typing now takes 2 seconds.

The Name

"Sesamo" comes from "Open Sesame" (Abrete Sesamo) - the magical phrase that opens doors. Just like the magic words, Sesamo opens the door to your Microsoft account instantly.

Open Source

Sesamo is built with transparency in mind. No ads, no tracking, no accounts. Just a simple tool that does one thing well.

코드는 화면에 표시되고, 휴대폰에서 조심스럽게 입력해야 합니다. 한 글자라도 틀리면? 처음부터 다시.

해결책

30초 걸리던 조심스러운 타이핑이 이제 2초면 끝납니다.
