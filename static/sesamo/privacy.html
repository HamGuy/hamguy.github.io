Privacy Policy

Overview

Sesamo is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

Sesamo does NOT collect:

  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Images or verification codes you scan

Camera & Storage Access

Sesamo requests camera permission solely for scanning verification codes in real-time. Images are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded or stored.

Storage access is used only to read images you explicitly select from your photo library.

Third-Party Services

Sesamo uses Google ML Kit for on-device text recognition. All processing happens locally on your device. No image data is sent to Google or any other server.

Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there is no data to secure or breach. Your verification codes remain on your device only.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: privacy@hamguy.xyz
